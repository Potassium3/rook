How to play

Move the chess rook to capture all of the pawns on the board.

Capture one pawn on each move.

Tap a pawn or use the arrows to move. To undo a move, tap the rook.

Tips

Reversing

Here the rook can reverse direction, capturing both pawns. However, rooks cannot jump.

3 in a row

Whenever three or more pieces are in a row, a decision is required. Choose carefully.

Last pawn

The pawn connected to only one other pawn is the last to be captured.

False finish

This pawn may not be the last pawn, as the rook can reverse direction to capture more.
